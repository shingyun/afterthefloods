After The Floods

What caused the water disasters around the world?

Dec. 2017 | Shing-Yun Chiang       Advisor | Graphicacy Team

This summer, people around the world were suffering from the floods caused by tropical cyclones. However, this year is not the most terrible year of floods, and tropical cyclone is not the major cause of floods either. From the data on large global flood events across 30 years, "heavy rain" comes out as the main factor historically and globally.

This project analyzes 4,500 floods from 1984 to Sep. 2017 around the world, and attempts to bring out the relationship between causes and impacts of floods in historical and geographical context.

Data Source: Dartmouth Flood Observatory
